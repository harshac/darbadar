Beyond the well

We have been trying to build a community(called btw - Beyond The Well) where travelers come and share their travel stories. This is not a commercial undertaking but a simple endeavor to encourage travel storytelling.

Think a bunch of people sitting around a campfire sharing stories minus the camping and the campfire and the bugs!
